4) Autism Training for First Responders – Sponsored by ASA
Objectives will include gaining the understanding of the effects of an emergency situation on a person with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and learning the steps to effectively handle emergency situations with a person with ASD. Includes an overview of ASD, and planning for these situations including how to effectively transfer a person with ASD to the hospital.
Where: 1100 San Leandro Blvd., Creekside Plaza, San Leandro, Redwood Conference Room
